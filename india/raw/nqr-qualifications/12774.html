Production Supervisor- Sewing

NQR Code:QG-05-AP-01777-2024-V2-AMHSSC

  • Old Code: QG-05-AP-01777-2024-V2-AMHSSC

    Copy
About this Qualification

Job Description

A Production Supervisor-Sewing guides activities of operators and/or other employees engaged in the process of manufacturing, inspection, processing and related activity. Production Supervisor plans and allocates resources and monitors schedules.

Eligibility Criteria

Criteria 1 Criteria 2 Experience Training Qualification
Diploma In relevant field 1 year None
UG In relevant field 3 years None
12th In relevant field 4 years None
Previous NSQF qualification of Level 4 3 years None
